Bulk RNAseq analysis of an asian diabetes cohort (GSE154881)

The analysis presented here has explored the transcriptome of whole blood Found at GSE154881 (This has not been published). These data contain the transcriptomic information from RNA isolated from whole blood in patients with type 2 diabetes, diabetic nephropathy and healthy controls.

This analysis will use the egdeR (Robinson, McCarthy, and Smyth 2010) work flow. We utilized two different methods of normalization “TMM” from edgeR and RUVg from RUVSeq(Risso et al. 2014). Enrichment analysis was then performed using GOseq (over representation analysis) (Robinson, McCarthy, and Smyth 2010) and gene set enrichment analysi using GSEA() from clusterProfiler (Xu et al. 2024).

References

Risso, Davide, John Ngai, Terence Speed, and Sandrine Dudoit. 2014. “Normalization of RNA-seq data using factor analysis of control genes or samples.” Nature Biotechnology 32 (9): 896–902. http://www.nature.com/nbt/journal/v32/n9/full/nbt.2931.html.
Robinson, Mark D, Davis J McCarthy, and Gordon K Smyth. 2010. “edgeR: A Bioconductor Package for Differential Expression Analysis of Digital Gene Expression Data.” Bioinformatics 26 (1): 139–40. https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btp616.
Xu, Shuangbin, Erqiang Hu, Yantong Cai, Zijing Xie, Xiao Luo, Li Zhan, Wenli Tang, et al. 2024. “Using clusterProfiler to Characterize Multiomics Data.” Nature Protocols, July. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41596-024-01020-z.
